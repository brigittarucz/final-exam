03.04.05 Superputin

In this theme I have learned how to collaborate with a big group of people for providing the best outcome. This project, Superputin , focused on making an animation ourselves from the very beginning and much planning was needed. Throughout the process I had the chance to use once again chaining events in Javascript, but this time, it was more complex with hiding and showing elements, as well as triggering audio once with the specific events.
